Chocolate Chip Cookies

Yield: 2 pounds 8 ounces

Equipment Needed:
 1 - Mixer
 1 - Paddle Attachment
 1 - Rubber Spatula
 1 - Ice Cream Scoop
 2 - Half-size Sheet Tray
 2 - Parchment Sheets

Ingedients:
 5 ounce Butter, unsalted and softened
 4 ounce Granulated Sugar
 4 ounce Light Brown Sugar
 4 grams Kosher Salt
 3 ounce Egg, whole
 5 grams Pure Vanilla Extract
 10 ounce All-purpose Flour
 4 grams Baking Soda
 10 ounce Chocolate Chips

Instructions:
 1) Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
 2) Add butter, sugars, and salt to mixing bowl, and mix until uniform.
 3) Mix in egg and vanilla extract a little bit at a time. Scrap sides of the bowl occasionally.
 4) Fold in chocolate chips.
 5)Scoop dough onto parchment lined sheet trays and bake for 10-12 minutes or until golden brown. Rotate tray about half way through baking time.


Notes:
Center will appear to be undercooked, the heat from the cookies will cook the cookie further and the chewy texture will be achieved.
