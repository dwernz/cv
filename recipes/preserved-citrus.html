Preserved Citrus

Yield: 1 bag

Equipment Needed:
 1 - Cutting Board
 1 - Chef's Knife
 1 - Measuring Spoon Set
 1 - Vacuum Seal Bag
 1 â€“ Vacuum Sealer

Ingedients:
 5 each Citrus Fruit (Lemon, Orange, Lime, etc.), quartered and seeds removed
 5 tablespoon Kosher Salt
 7 tablespoon Granulated Sugar

Instructions:
 1) Combine all ingredients in vacuum seal bag, and seal.
 2) Preserve for a minimum of 3 weeks.
