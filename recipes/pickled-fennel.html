Pickled Fennel

Yield: One Quart

Equipment Needed:
 1 - Mandolin
 1 - Saucepot
 1 - Cutting Board
 1 - Vegetable Peeler
 1 - Measuring Spoons and Cup Set
 1 - Chef Knife

Ingedients:
 3/4 cup Tarragon Vinegar
 3/8 cup Water
 2 1/2 tablespoons Granulated Sugar
 1 tablespoon Kosher Salt
 1/2 teaspoon Fennel Seeds
 1 teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper Flakes
 1 each Star Anise Pod
 1-inch strip Orange Zest
 1 tablespoon Tarragon, fresh and minced
 1 each Fennel Bulb, shaved thinly

Instructions:
 1) Put fennel and tarragon in a container. Set aside.
 2) Add remaining ingredients into a saucepot. Over high heat, bring to a boil. Reduce to a simmer and heat until sugar is dissolved.
 3) Pour pickling liquid over fennel and tarragon. Do not cover, let cool.
 4) Cover and refrigerate. Wait at least 30 minutes before consuming.
