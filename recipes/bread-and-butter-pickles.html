Bread and Butter Pickles

Yield: 5-Pint Jars

Equipment Needed:
 5 - Pint Canning Jars
 1 - 16 qt Canning Pot
 1 - Jar Lifter
 1 - Chef Knife
 1 - Cutting Board
 1 - Measuring Spoon and Cup Set
 1 - Large Bowl

Ingedients:
 2 1/2 pounds Kirby Cucumbers
 1 pounds Yellow Onions, thinly sliced
 1/4 cup Kosher Salt
 1 1/4 cup White Distilled Vinegar
 1 1/4 cup Apple Cider Vinegar
 2 1/4 cup Sugar
 1 tablespoon Mustard Seeds
 1 teaspoon Crushed Red Pepper
 3/4 teaspoon Celery Seeds
 1 each Cinnamon Stick
 6 each Allspice Berries
 6 each Cloves
 1/2 teaspoon Turmeric

Instructions:
 1. Prep the cucumbers. Carefully rinse the cucumbers. Slice cucumbers 1/4 inch thick and place in a large bowl.
 2. Toss sliced cucumbers and onions with salt and cover with ice. Chill 4 hours, rinse and drain.
 3. Sterilize jars.
 4. Boil vinegar, sugar, and spices. Add the cucumbers and onions and bring to a boil again. Transfer to jars.
 5. Seal properly.
